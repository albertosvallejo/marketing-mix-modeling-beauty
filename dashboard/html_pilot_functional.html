Why are there two baseline figures? ▾
The two figures measure different counterfactuals. The log-space value (89.5%) is computed on the transformed scale the Bayesian model uses internally — useful for assessing model fit and diagnosing overfitting. The counterfactual value (26.0%) answers the business question: "what revenue would we retain if all paid media were switched off tomorrow?" — that is the number for budget decisions. Both are correct; they measure different things. Reporting only one misleads either technical reviewers or business stakeholders, depending on which is omitted.
